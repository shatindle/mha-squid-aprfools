    About

    Through a twist of fate, Class 1-A finds themselves thrust into a post-apocalyptic future where Squids who can transform into Kids roam the streets, obsessed with the latest fashion. Stumbling into Marie, the class learns that DJ Octavio has once again stolen the Great Zap fish! As the only source of power strong enough to return Deku and his friends home, can our heroes band together with Agent 3 and Agent 8 in time to stop DJ Octavio and his minions?
